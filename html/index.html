How does it work?

STEP 0

The Bubble team will hand over a Bubble bag made just for you!

STEP 1

Choose a minimum of two days in a week. For example, Monday & Sunday. At the beginning of every month, the Bubble team will notify you about the item of that month. For example, rice.

STEP 2

Add a fistful of rice into you Bubble bag on those two selected days (Monday & Sunday) every week.

STEP 3

At the end of the month, on the day of the collection drive, the Bubble team will visit your house with a gunny bag into which you can pour your contributions.

STEP 4

The Bubble team will then donate these collections to an orphanage in need.
